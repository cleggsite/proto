Make Eggs Over Easy The Right Way

  1. Turn on your cooktop (heat it a little under medium power) and get your pan out.
  2. Put ~.5 tbsp of butter in the pan and distribute the butter with your spatula.
  3. Let the butter chunk melt until not visible.
  4. Crack your eggs and let the yolks drop at a low height over the pan. Don't crack them!
  5. Wait until egg membrane becomes a solid white.
  6. Time to flip! Position your spatula against the edge of the egg and swiftly thrust it under the membrane.
  7. You can now flip the egg.
  8. Allow the egg to cook a little while longer but don't let the yolk harden.
  9. Plate the eggs.
